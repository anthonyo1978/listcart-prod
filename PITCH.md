**Stop juggling vendors. Start closing deals faster.**

Every property listing needs the same thing: great photos, compelling copy, signage, styling. But coordinating these service providers? That's where agents lose hours every week.

**The Problem:**
Sarah (typical agent) needs to list 123 Main Street. She emails 5 photographers, 3 copywriters, 2 sign companies. She waits. Follows up. Waits more. Compares quotes in scattered email threads. Loses track of who said yes. Vendors ask the same questions repeatedly. She's spending 4-6 hours per listing just coordinating services.

**Enter ListCart.**

Sarah creates a ListCart in 2 minutes. She picks her services, adds property details once, and sends a single link to her trusted vendor network. 

Now the magic happens:

- **All conversations in ONE place** - No more email chaos. WhatsApp-style chat threads keep every negotiation organized and visible.
- **Real-time collaboration** - Vendors respond with quotes, availability, and questions. Sarah can see everything at a glance.
- **Instant approvals** - One click to accept quotes. No more "Let me check and get back to you" emails.
- **Automated invoicing** - Once approved, invoices generate automatically with transparent pricing.
- **Complete audit trail** - Every conversation, quote, and decision documented for compliance.

**The Result:**
That 4-6 hour coordination nightmare? Now 20 minutes. Sarah books more listings because she's not drowning in admin. Vendors love it because they get all the information upfront. Property owners see professionalism and speed.

**The Business Model:**
Small platform fee per transaction. Agents gain hours back. Vendors get more consistent work. Everyone wins.

**ListCart: Where listing services meet simplicity.**
