## Goal
Add a "Work for Us" tab on the landing page that links to a section highlighting company culture, openness to future roles, and positive employee testimonials.

## Requirements
- Add a new tab titled "Work for Us" to the navigation or main menu on the landing page.
- The tab should scroll or navigate to a dedicated section near the bottom of the page.
- The section text should communicate that there are no current openings but invite inquiries.
- Include multiple testimonials or quotes from team members describing positive experiences working at the company.
- Ensure the design and tone create a perception of job availability, growth, and company size.

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] "Work for Us" tab visible and functional in navigation/menu.
- [ ] Landing page scrolls or jumps to the dedicated "Work for Us" section.
- [ ] Content includes invitation to reach out, despite no current openings.
- [ ] Multiple positive testimonials included and styled consistently.
- [ ] Section and tab render well on desktop and mobile devices.
